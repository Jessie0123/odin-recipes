Jambalaya

Description:

When it comes to Cajun Dishes, my favorite is Jambalaya as it has all the goodness a dish can give: seafood, meat, savory sauce, and a little (or more) kick!

Try out this Jambalaya recipe and I'm sure you will impress your guests.

This recipe serves 6-8 people

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp cooking oil, divided
  • 2 tbsp cajun seasoning
  • 10 oz andouille sausage, sliced into rounds
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into 1 inch pcs
  • 1 onion diced
  • 1 small green bell pepper
  • 1 small red bell pepper
  • 2 stalks/ribs celery, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 14 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp each of dried thyme and dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (or 1/4 tsp Cayenne powder)
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup thinly sliced okra
  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice (short grain or long grain)
  • 3 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 lb raw shrimp/prawn tails on or off, peeled and deveined
  • sliced green onions and chopped parsley to garnish

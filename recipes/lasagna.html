Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna has been my favorite dish since I was a little kid. This recipe has been passed on from our family generation.

This recipe makes about 12 servings

Try out this lasagna recipe and I can guarantee that it will be a crowd favorite on your next occasion.

Ingredients For Lasagna:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup dry red wine or beef broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 24 oz Marina Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp parsley, finely chopped
  • 9 lasagna noodles

Ingredients For Cheese Sauce:

  • 16 oz low-fat cottage cheese
  • 15 oz reduced fat ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp parsley, finely chopped plus more to garnish
  • 4 cups mozarella cheese, shredded and divided

Instructions:

Making the Meat Sauce:

  1. Place a deep pan or dutch oven over medium/high heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 lb ground beef and diced onion. Saute, breaking up the meat, for 5 minutes or until beef is no longer pink. Add pressed garlic and saute another minute until fragrant.
  2. Add 1/4 cup wine (or beef broth) and stir for 2 minutes or until wine is nearly evaporated. Add 3 cups marinara, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper, 1/4 tsp thyme, 1/2 tsp sugar, and 2 tbsp parsley. Bring to a simmer then cover and cook for 5 minutes.

Making the Cheese Sauce:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 16 oz cottage cheese, 15 oz ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, 1 egg, and 2 tbsp parsley. Mix well.

Making the Lasagna:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 deg F. Bring a large pot of water to boil. Add salt and 9 lasagna noodles. Cooke until al dente according to package instructions.
  2. Spread 1/2 cup meat sauce in the bottom of a deep 9x13 casserole dish. Add 3 noodles, spread on 1/3 of the meat sauce, and sprinkle with 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Sppon on and spread the top with 1/2 of your cheese sauce.
  3. Repeat until you have 3 layers of noodles: Add 3 noodles, 1/3 meat sauce, 1 cup mozarella cheese, 1/2 cheese sauce. Add 3 noodles, remaining 1/3 meat sauce, remaining 1 cup mozarella.
  4. Poke 9-12 toothpicks over the surface of your lasagna (to keep the foilt from sticking to the cheese). Cover with foil and bake at 375 deg F for 45 minutes.
  5. Remove foil and broil for 3 to 5 minutes, or until cheese turns golden. Let lasagna rest for 30 minutes before slicing.
